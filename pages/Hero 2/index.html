GOD FIST LEE SIN

"The Man Climbed the Mountain for the Third Time"

He remembered his first ascent perfectly, although he was just a boy when he first reached the mythical peak in search of the God Fist. But the God Fist deemed him unworthy of any training and told the boy that he must first defeat each master in each school. Once he had done so, the boy could fight the God Fist.
